Porting data
------------
1. Export from old db: sqlite3 old.db
    - .mode insert
    - .output file.sql
    - select NULL,COLUMNS_IN_CORRECT_ORDER,datetime(),datetime() from TABLE;
2. Search and replace "INTO table" with "INTO real_table_name"
3. Import to new db: sqlite3 new.db
    - .read file.sql
